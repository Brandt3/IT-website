Why Join Us?

The SMU IT Club is a student-run organization dedicated to fostering technical skills, professional development, and community among tech enthusiasts. Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, there's a place for you here.

  • Hands-on workshops and coding challenges
  • Networking with industry professionals
  • Collaborative projects and hackathons

How to Join

Joining the IT Club is simple and free for all SMU students. There are two easy ways to get involved:

  1. Show up to a meeting — We meet every Monday at 6:00 P.M. in La Salle Hall, Room 144. Just walk in!
  2. Send us an email — Reach out to cxhafn21@smumn.edu and we'll get you connected.

No prior experience required — everyone is welcome.

Meeting Details

Our weekly meetings are the heartbeat of the club. Each session is packed with engaging content, member-led demos, guest speakers, and open collaboration time.

When: Every Monday at 6:00 P.M.
Where: La Salle Hall, Room 144
What to bring: Your laptop and curiosity!
