Planned topics

The planned topics for the SMU IT Club include hands‑on workshops, collaborative projects, and discussions on emerging technologies that help students build practical skills. The club also plans to host guest speakers and peer‑learning sessions to support members at all experience levels.

Completed

Completed topics include introductory coding sessions, cybersecurity basics, and a walkthrough of essential IT tools used on campus. Members have also participated in collaborative troubleshooting activities that strengthened teamwork and problem‑solving skills.
